#### Quantum Entanglement and Consciousness: Research Insights
Research suggests that quantum entanglement could play a role in brain function, particularly in relation to consciousness. An article from The Quantum Insider ([Researchers Explore Quantum Entanglement's Potential Role in Neural Synchronization](https://thequantuminsider.com/2024/08/03/researchers-explore-quantum-entanglements-potential-role-in-neural-synchronization/)) notes that most scientists believe the brain's warm, wet, and noisy environment is inimical to quantum effects, with physicist Max Tegmark arguing it’s unlikely for quantum mechanics to play a meaningful role in cognition. Notable figures like Roger Penrose and Stuart Hameroff have suggested that microtubules in neurons could host quantum effects, with consciousness arising from the collapse of quantum superpositions, as discussed in a 2024 Scientific American article ([Experiments Prepare to Test Whether Consciousness Arises from Quantum Weirdness](https://www.scientificamerican.com/article/experiments-prepare-to-test-whether-consciousness-arises-from-quantum/)).
